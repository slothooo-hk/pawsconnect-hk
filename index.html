About PawsConnect HK

PawsConnect HK is Hong Kong's premier centralized platform for pet adoption, volunteering, and animal welfare support. We bridge the gap between rescue organizations and the community, making it easier than ever to find your perfect companion or contribute to animal welfare.

How It Works

1

For Adopters

Browse pets from multiple rescue organizations in one place. Filter by species, size, age, and location to find your perfect match.

2

For Organizations

Showcase your animals to a wider audience. Manage applications and connect with potential adopters efficiently.

3

For Volunteers

Discover volunteer opportunities across Hong Kong. Find roles that match your schedule and interests.
